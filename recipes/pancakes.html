Pancake Recipe

Ingredient list:

  • Flour (1 cup)

  • Sugar (2 tbsp)

  • Baking powder (2 tbsp)

  • Salt (1 tbsp or to taste)

  • Milk (1 cup)

  • Vegetable oil (2 tbsp)

  • Egg (1 unit)

Steps:

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl

  2. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, oil, and egg

  3. Mix until smooth

  4. Pour batter on a slighly oiled pan heated over medium-high heat

  5. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry,

  6. Serve with toppings of choice
